What will be — the 12–18 month read

Dated, near-term shifts you can plan against — not vibes.

Now · Co-lending economics reset

The Co-Lending Directions (in force Jan 2026) extend co-lending to all lending and add minimum-retention rules — changing originator capital economics. [Co-Lending 2026]

Now · BNPL formalised

RBI's late-2025 BNPL guidance treats it as regulated lending; >₹50,000 cr outstanding now sits under oversight. [RBI BNPL 2025]

6–12 mo · AA-native underwriting becomes default

GST, UPI and supply-chain flows feed credit decisions; underwriting compresses from ~3 days to ~3 hours. [KPMG · AA MD]

12–18 mo · Public-market test

KreditBee's pre-IPO round signals the first wave of digital-lending IPOs — a valuation reset for the category. [Fintech News SG]
